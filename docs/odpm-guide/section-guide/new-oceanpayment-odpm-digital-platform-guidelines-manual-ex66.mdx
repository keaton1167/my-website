# Preface:

## Overview of ODPM Digital Platform Multi Terminal

The ODPM digital platform caters to the multi terminal usage scenarios of merchants, launching PC, H5 mobile, and WeChat mini program. The PC end focuses on overall business operation management, the H5 mobile end focuses on business information query, the mini program focuses on the financial section, data analysis, and message notification. Overviews of each terminal page, functional modules, and functional buttons are detailed below:
